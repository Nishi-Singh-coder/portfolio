The long version, in short.

I'm a strategy & operations professional with 9+ years delivering data-driven transformation, operating model design, and performance improvement across regulated and complex sectors.

My work tends to start with a question: how do we make this actually work at scale? I care about operating models that hold up under pressure, processes that don't quietly break in production, and analytics that lead to decisions instead of decks.

I've worked across energy (Sizewell C), healthcare (Johnson & Johnson), FMCG (global Fortune-level CPG and beverages clients), and retail (J.C. Penney, SPAR, Spencer's). The common thread: large, matrixed environments where the biggest leverage often comes from clarity rather than complexity.

I'm currently a Strategy & Transformation consultant at Capgemini Invent in London, leading operating model design and governance work for global clients. I started my career in retail planning in India, moved through Johnson & Johnson and Capgemini Mumbai, and have been in London since 2023.
